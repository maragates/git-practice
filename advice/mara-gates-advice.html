Hi, my name is Mara and I have some advice for you!

Failure is a natural part of the learning process.

It's okay to make mistakes; they help you understand concepts better. This is a normal part of the process of learning a new technology, and in fact, it's a critical part of the process.

Coding takes time to master.

Don't rush through it. Focus on understanding the fundamentals and building a strong foundation.

Patience is key.

Learning to code can be challenging. Be patient with yourself and celebrate small victories along the way.

Experimentation is encouraged.

Don't be afraid to try new things and explore different approaches. It's through experimentation that you'll discover your unique coding style.
